Bins
Centralized Zero Waste Collection Bins
A Centralized Zero Waste Collection Bin is a large cabinet set that contains waste bins for the four different waste streams; mixed paper, bottles and cans, compost and landfill.
Outdoor Bins
These outdoor bins are used in outside areas on campus for mixed paper, bottles and cans and landfill waste.
Slim Jim Sets
The standard bins used when CRRS implements composting and recycling in a campus building. A ‘set’ of slim jims includes compost, landfill, mixed paper, and bottles & cans. The bins have lids to contain smells, keep out pests, and discourage contamination.
Mini Bin
Office desk-side bins that are comprised of a mixed paper recycling bin and a small landfill saddle bin. Having a small landfill bin encourages office occupants to use centralized composting and recycling bins.
Dumpsters
Dumpsters are used for the storage of waste from each building and are located outside in an enclosure area.
